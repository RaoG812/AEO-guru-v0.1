Operational storyline

Signal-driven workflow

Embeddings-first
1 · Ingest

Submit root domains, sitemaps, and curated URL lists. Every document is embedded, tagged with metadata, and assigned to a project so you can re-run crawls or append fresh data.

  • Form-driven intake with validation and status messaging.

  • Project-aware so teams can maintain multiple sites in parallel.

2 · Cluster

Group embeddings into an intent-labelled semantic core, surface canonical questions, and list content gaps before they impact AI Overviews.

  • Opportunity scores, recommended schema blocks, and intent colors.

  • Manual "Enlarge core" runs pull in fresh crawl intelligence.

3 · Outputs

Preview the most relevant clusters, check attribute deltas, then trigger downloads when the cockpit's overrides look perfect.

  • Status pills track ingestion, clustering, download, and projects.

  • Ghost buttons expose Semantic core, JSON-LD, GEO, and robots exports.
